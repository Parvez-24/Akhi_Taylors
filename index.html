Our Story

Craftsmanship
in Every Thread

Founded in 2005, Akhi Taylors was born from a deep love of fabric, form, and the art of dressing well. What started as a small corner shop has grown into Chittagong's most trusted name in bespoke tailoring.

We specialize in blending traditional techniques with modern silhouettes — whether you need a sharp business suit, a formal sherwani, or an everyday shalwar kameez crafted with care.

Precision

Every measurement taken to the millimeter for a flawless fit.

Heritage

Rooted in traditional tailoring passed down through generations.

Quality

Only the finest imported and local fabrics make the cut.

Service

Personal attention from first consultation to final fitting.
